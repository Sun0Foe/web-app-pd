Attack on Titan

Genre: Action, Drama, Fantasy

In a world ravaged by towering man-eating Titans, humanity hides behind massive walls to survive. But when one of the walls is breached, a young boy named Eren Yeager vows to destroy every last Titan. What follows is a dark, intense journey full of war, secrets, and survival, where nothing is as it seems.

Death Note

Genre: Mystery, Psychological, Supernatural, Thriller

When genius high schooler Light Yagami finds a mysterious notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it, he sets out to rid the world of criminals. But his actions attract the attention of a brilliant detective named L, sparking a tense battle of wits, morality, and identity.

Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

Genre: Action, Adventure, Drama, Fantasy

After a failed attempt to bring their mother back to life using alchemy, brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric suffer devastating consequences. Determined to restore what they lost, they embark on a powerful journey filled with mystery, government corruption, and deep philosophical themes about life and humanity.
